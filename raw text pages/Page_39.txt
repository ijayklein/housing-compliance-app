## Title
* Noise-producing equipment site planning

General rules (verbatim bullets on page)
* May not be located in front, rear, or interior-side yards.
* May encroach into street-side yard up to 6' (**Figure 27** C below)
* All service equip. must meet the City’s Noise Ordinance in Chapter 9.10 of the Municipal Code.
* Replacement of equipment shall conform to this section where feasible.
* Must be insulated and housed, however, Planning Dept. can waive this requirement if equipment is located in buildable area and a combination of noise specifications, location, and/or other screening or buffering will assure compliance with the City’s Noise Ordinance at the nearest lot line.
